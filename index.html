About Me:

My name is Eric Hopkins, I live in Redford, MI.

I'm currently just 3 months out from graduating from Schoolcraft College. I've had a life long love for computers and technology, building computers and fiddling with hardware. In 2010, I started learning to develop software. I was first taught on .NET, but have become more fond of Java and other platform agnostic languages.


I first got excited about software development because I thought the idea of making video games was unbelievably cool. I've since fallen in love with the process more than the end product.


I'm seriously passionate about what I do, I find it legitimately fun. I believe that along with constant work, one of the best ways to get better is to put what I do out for all to see. I upload everything I do now to GitHub. I'm constantly growing, and looking to not just get; but earn my place.

Along with going to school, I'm a big fan of learning on my own time.

The classroom is great, but it's not enough. Education doesn't end with school, especially with the ever expanding world of tech.


If you want to see what I'm reading a learning about right now, just click the link above.


Outside of computers and technology, I'm a huge history buff. When I first started college back in 2008, it's what I went in for. I even ended up getting an Associate of Arts degree out of it. I realized however, that it wasn't my calling.


I still love reading and learning about it on my own time. My personal favorite period is early medieval europe; but I would challenge anyone to find a time or place they couldn't get me excited about.
